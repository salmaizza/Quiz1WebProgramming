HOMETOWN
Banyuwangi

Banyuwangi is the largest district on the island of Java, located in the easternmost part of the island of Java. No wonder Banyuwangi is very rich in culture and tourist attractions. Banyuwangi Regency until now continues to intensively make improvements in terms of regional development. Development is carried out in various sectors, ranging from education, health, tourism, agriculture, MSMEs, and infrastructure. Developments in the infrastructure sector make inter-regional accessibility to Banyuwangi easier and shorter with the construction of the airport. As a form of accountability, the Banyuwangi Regency Government's financial reports also continue to be improved. Banyuwangi has a native, namely Osing tribe. Osing tribe also has its own language, namely Osing language and the majority population in several sub-districts in Banyuwangi Regency. The Osing tribe is a blend of culture and tradition in Banyuwangi. Therefore, I, as a local Banyuwangi resident, are very proud, wanting to introduce various culinary tours and tourist attractions.
