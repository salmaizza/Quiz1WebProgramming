HOMETOWN
Banyuwangi

Banyuwangi is the largest district on the island of Java, located in the easternmost part of the island of Java. No wonder Banyuwangi is very rich in culture and tourist attractions. Banyuwangi Regency until now continues to intensively make improvements in terms of regional development. Development is carried out in various sectors, ranging from education, health, tourism, agriculture, MSMEs, and infrastructure. Developments in the infrastructure sector make inter-regional accessibility to Banyuwangi easier and shorter with the construction of the airport. As a form of accountability, the Banyuwangi Regency Government's financial reports also continue to be improved. Banyuwangi has a native, namely Osing tribe. Osing tribe also has its own language, namely Osing language and the majority population in several sub-districts in Banyuwangi Regency. The Osing tribe is a blend of culture and tradition in Banyuwangi. Therefore, I, as a local Banyuwangi resident, are very proud, wanting to introduce various culinary tours and tourist attractions.

Discover the most
attractive foods

Sego Tempong

Sego Tempong is the most famous traditional food from Banyuwangi. The dish served with various side dishes, fresh vegetables and chili sauce. The hallmark of Sego Tempong is its spicy chili sauce.

Rujak Soto

Rujak Soto is a typical dish from Banyuwangi. This dish is a unique blend Of vegetable salad with Soto. There is an ingridients Of soto as well as a taste of rujak with the aroma Of the shrimp paste.

Botok Tawon

In Banyuwangi Has A Special Culinary With Honeycomb Ingredients Called Botok Tawon. Botok Tawon Are Often Served When Carrying Out Traditional Rituals.

Pecel Pitik

Pecel Pitik is a culinary dish with the basic ingredients of shredded free-range chicken and smeared with grated coconut. Flavored with candlenut, cayenne pepper, and shrimp paste.

Sego Cawuk

Sego Cawuk is a typical breakfast menu. Sego Cawuk is served using banana leaves. This food consists of rice mixed with grated coconut sauce and roasted young corn shavings, and pindang fish sauce.

Pecel Rawon

Pecel Rawon dish that combines vegetable pecel and rawon meat in one bowl. In Rawon Pecel, you can see rice soaked in rawon sauce. Complementary vegetable pecel doused with peanut sauce.
